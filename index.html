We then cluster our numbers

  • We use agglomerative hierarchical clustering using Ward-linkage

  • We cut the dendrogram at a certain depth to decide what our clusters are

  • The downstream process reclusters and each time expands this depth which builds the hierarchy (more on that later)

We got out clusters, now what?

The Labelling

Well, we're here now what do we do. We've made a cut of the tree, we have what we think are homogenous branches and leaves.Can we give a name to them automatically?

Each cluster is then send through a selection process that selects and appropriate label for the cluster if it can!
